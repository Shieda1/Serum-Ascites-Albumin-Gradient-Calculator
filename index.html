What is the serum ascites albumin gradient?
The serum ascites albumin gradient is a representative of the albumin concentration difference between blood serum and the ascitic fluid.
How to calculate serum ascites albumin gradient?
To calculate serum ascites albumin gradient you should use the following formula:
serum ascites albumin gradient = serum albumin concentration - ascitic fluid albumin concentration
